The Case of the Sneaky Vandal: A Detective Hunter Mystery

Role: Lead Programmer

Unity, C#

A detective game solving a series of thefts on a cruise ship, created in partnership with the American Student Association. The game aims to test time management and deductive reasoning skills in kids around ages 8-12. Key systems and functionality I implemented include a time system affecting NPC locations and availability and allowing time skips, NPC dialogue, NPC pathing, a journal with automatic note-taking upon discoveries, and a rotation-based wire puzzle.

Status: Coming Soon!

VR Human Benchmark Tests

Role: Co-Creator

Unity, C#

A VR hub with human benchmark tests, allowing users to gauge their cognitive capabilities in areas such as reaction speed and memory in the immersive world of VR. Personal improvement can be measured over time in areas such as accuracy, speed, and memory. Current tests include a basic reaction speed test and a Simon puzzle. Additional features include score-saving, username input for score organization, and score view sorting by highest, lowest, and most recent scores.

Status: Demo

Balloon Tower Defense Mini Game

Role: Creator

p5, JavaScript

A tower defense game based on Ninja Kiwi's Bloons Tower Defense, created entirely in JavaScript. The goal of the game is to place towers to pop the invading balloons. Difficulty increases as balloons of different colors appear. A twist is added to the original game with two-player mode, allowing friends to compete for points from popping balloons while sharing the same number of lives. The play button in the top-left corner of the hosting site can be pressed to try out the game.

Status: Complete
